About Our Church

Learn about our mission, vision, and the community we've built together.

Our Mission

Grace Community Church exists to love God, love people, and make disciples of Jesus Christ. We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the transformative power of God's grace.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant community of faith that impacts our city and beyond through relevant teaching, authentic worship, and compassionate service. We envision a church where broken lives are restored, families are strengthened, and individuals discover their God-given purpose.

Our History

Founded in 1985, Grace Community Church began as a small gathering of believers meeting in a home. Over the decades, we've grown into a diverse congregation of over 500 members, while maintaining our commitment to intimate community and personal relationships.

Our Beliefs

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God and our final authority
  • There is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • Jesus Christ is God's only begotten Son, conceived by the Holy Spirit
  • Salvation is received through faith in Jesus Christ alone
  • The church is the body of Christ, called to serve and love one another
