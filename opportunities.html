Serve With Us

Grace Community Church is always looking for passionate individuals who want to use their God-given talents to serve our congregation and community. Whether you're a musician, photographer, administrator, or have other skills, we have opportunities for you to make a difference.

Current Openings

Worship Keyboard Player

Part-time

Department: Worship & Creative Arts

Schedule: Sundays, Wednesday rehearsals, special events

Compensation: $150-200 per service (based on experience)

We are seeking a skilled keyboard player to join our worship team. The ideal candidate should have experience with contemporary Christian music, be able to read chord charts, and work well in a team environment.

Requirements:
  • Proficient in piano/keyboard with 3+ years experience
  • Familiar with contemporary worship music (Hillsong, Elevation, Bethel, etc.)
  • Ability to play by ear and read chord charts
  • Strong team collaboration skills
  • Committed Christian with heart for worship
  • Available for Sunday services and Wednesday rehearsals

Church Photographer

Freelance/Contract

Department: Communications & Media

Schedule: Sundays, special events, occasional weekdays

Compensation: $75-125 per hour (based on assignment)

Looking for a talented photographer to capture meaningful moments during our services, events, and community activities. This role is perfect for someone who can tell stories through images and understands the sacred nature of church environments.

Requirements:
  • Professional photography experience with portfolio
  • Own professional camera equipment
  • Experience with event photography
  • Understanding of church etiquette and respectful approach
  • Photo editing skills (Lightroom, Photoshop)
  • Ability to deliver edited photos within 48 hours

Sound Engineer/Technician

Part-time

Department: Worship & Production

Schedule: Sundays, special events

Compensation: $125-175 per service (based on experience)

Seeking an experienced sound engineer to manage our audio systems during services and events. Must have technical expertise and a good ear for balanced, clear sound in a worship environment.

Requirements:
  • Experience with live sound mixing
  • Knowledge of digital mixing consoles (Behringer, Yamaha, etc.)
  • Understanding of church audio needs
  • Available Sunday mornings and occasional evenings
  • Problem-solving skills for technical issues

Children's Ministry Coordinator

Part-time

Department: Children's Ministry

Schedule: Sundays, Wednesday evenings, planning meetings

Compensation: $20-25 per hour, 15-20 hours/week

Lead and coordinate our children's ministry programs, ensuring safe, engaging, and spiritually enriching experiences for kids ages 3-12. This role involves curriculum planning, volunteer coordination, and direct interaction with children and families.

Requirements:
  • Experience working with children in educational or ministry setting
  • Background check and child safety training required
  • Organizational and leadership skills
  • Passion for children's spiritual development
  • Available weekends and some evenings

Administrative Assistant

Part-time

Department: Church Administration

Schedule: Monday-Friday, 20 hours/week flexible

Compensation: $18-22 per hour

Support the daily operations of our church office with administrative tasks, member communication, and event coordination. Perfect for someone with strong organizational skills and a heart for service.

Requirements:
  • Administrative experience preferred
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office and Google Workspace
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality
  • Detail-oriented and organized
